CustomerHub Finance — Vendor Payment Policy (v3.1)

Payment Terms:
- Standard vendors: Net 30 from invoice date
- Strategic partners: Net 45 with 2% early payment discount (10 days)
- International vendors: Net 60, wire transfer only, currency hedging applied

Approval Thresholds:
- Under $5,000: Department manager approval
- $5,000 - $25,000: Finance director approval
- $25,000 - $100,000: VP Finance approval + budget code verification
- Over $100,000: CFO approval + board notification

Invoice Processing:
1. Vendor submits invoice to ap@customerhub.example.com
2. AP team validates against PO and receiving records (2 business days)
3. Three-way match: PO, invoice, goods receipt
4. Discrepancies routed to procurement for resolution
5. Approved invoices queued for next payment cycle (weekly, Fridays)

Expense Reimbursement:
- Receipts required for all expenses over $25
- Mileage rate: $0.67/mile (IRS 2024 standard)
- Per diem: $75/day domestic, $120/day international
- Submission deadline: 30 days from expense date

Department: Finance
Classification: Internal Use Only
